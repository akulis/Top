To Place An Order

To place an order, simply pick your items and quantities, add to your shopping card and proceed through the checkout process. Please note that each order is custom and will require production time. All of the items featured on this site are made-to-order just for you, so we appreciate your understanding that returns and exchanges are not accepted except in the case of defective product.

Sizing

Each product features a size chart to help you select the size best suitable for you.

Shipping Options

All orders will be shipped to your home using standard shipping rates.
