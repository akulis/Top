Placing an Order

To order merchandise, add selected items and quantities to your shopping card and proceed through the checkout process.

Stock items will ship within 3 business days.

Please allow 10 working days for production on custom orders.

Returns

Stock items may be returned for any reason within 30 days of original shipping. Returns must be in new, unworn, unlaundered condition. Download the return form below, fill out and include with returned product. We appreciate your understanding that custom orders may only be returned in the case of defect.
