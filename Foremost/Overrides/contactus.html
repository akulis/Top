To Place an Order

To order merchandise, add selected items and quantities to your shopping card and proceed through the checkout process.

All items are non-stock and are produced twice per month.

Orders placed between the 1st and 15th of each month will ship around the 1st business day of the next month.

Orders placed between the 16th and end of the month will ship around the 15th of the next month.

Returns

All of the items featured on this site are made-to-order just for you, so we appreciate your understanding that returns and exchanges are not accepted except in the case of defective product.

Questions?

Contact Customer Service at webservices@toppromotions.com or 608-826-6990, if you have a question about your order, please have your order # available.
