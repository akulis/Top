To Place an Order

To place an order, simply pick your items and quantities along with logo choice, add to your shopping card and proceed through the checkout process. Please note that each order is custom and will require production time. All of the items featured on this site are made-to-order just for you, so we appreciate your understanding that returns and exchanges are not accepted except in the case of defective product.

Orders placed the 1st through the 15th of the month will ship around the 1st business day of the following month. Orders placed the 16th through end of the month will ship around the 15th of the following month.

For questions regarding the website or an existing order please contact Top Promotions Customer Service at webservices@toppromotions.com or 608-826-6990.
