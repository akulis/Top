Return/Exchange Information

Contact Customer Service toll free at 877-742-5686 for ALL returns/exchanges of product. Once your return/exchange has been authorized, CLICK HERE to access a copy of our return/exchange form. Complete the form and return with the items(s) you need to return/exchange. Once we have received the items accompanied by the form, we will process your return/exchange.

We will ONLY accept returns or exchanges of non-discontinued/closeout items within 30 days of the receipt of your order.

Shipping Rates and Policies

All online orders are shipped via UPS Ground shipping or US Postal Service. Your rates are calculated based on the weight of your shipment and the current freight rates from these carriers. If you select an expedited ship method, please be aware that this does not expedite the order processing time frames below, if you need an order processed as a rush, please contact customer service and we will try to accommodate your request.

Orders containing stock only will ship within 3 working days. Orders containing custom/non-inventory items ship within 10 working days (please note the day the order is submitted does not count).

If an order contains both stock and custom/non-inventory product, the entire order will be shipped within 10 working days. If you need the stock items shipped early, you must place as two separate orders, one for stock and one for non-inventory. If a stock item is currently out of stock (back ordered), the back ordered item will ship as soon as it becomes available (usually within 10-12 business days). Item descriptions will indicate if an item is stock or non-inventory.

Our Guarantee

We want to you have an enjoyable shopping experience. If you find there is a problem with your order, please contact Customer Service toll-free at 877-742-5686.

Security

We take great precautions to protect the integrity of the credit card information you submit on this site. You can feel assured that when you enter your credit card information you will go into a secured session, at which point our server will encrypt (or scramble) the credit card data so that it cannot be read in transit. For added security, your credit card information will not be stored.

Changing Orders

All orders are processed immediately after confirmation, so submitted orders are considered final. We will try to accommodate order changes or cancelation requests, but cannot guarantee our ability to complete them.

Contact Customer Service toll-free at 1-877-742-5686 for order changes or cancelation requests. All change requests are subject to additional charges which will be quoted and billed separately.
