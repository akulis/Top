To Place an Order

To place an order, simply pick your items and quantities, add to your shopping cart and proceed through the checkout process. Please note that each order is custom and will require production time. All of the items featured on this site are made-to-order just for you, so we appreciate your understanding that returns and exchanges are not accepted except in the case of defective product.

Sizing

Each product features a size chart to help you select the size best suitable for you.

Shipping Options

If you select to have your order shipped you will be billed standard shipping rates for the carrier selected.

For questions regarding the website or an existing order please contact Top Promotions Customer Service at webservices@toppromotions.com or 608-826-6990.
